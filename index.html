🧠 Neural Network Architecture
Deep feedforward ANN with ELU activations, BatchNormalization, Dropout regularisation. Adam optimiser + EarlyStopping (patience=60) on 240 paper-derived samples.
⚠️ Dataset & Model Notes

✅ Dataset: 240 rows from paper RSM (Yıldız et al. 2023) — FA, AA, PA × CCD + dense grid

✅ Balanced: 80 samples per acid type; continuous coverage from low to high efficiency

✅ ANN vs dataset size: With 240 rows ANN may underperform vs RF/XGBoost. R² shown after training.

✅ Callbacks: EarlyStopping + ReduceLROnPlateau prevent overfitting

✅ Recommendation: If ANN R² < 0.92, prefer GPR or XGBoost for predictions
